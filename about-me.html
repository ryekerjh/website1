Oh, you thought you knew?

For longer than I care to divulge, I have worked retail and food service, and for the most part those industries have defined my possibilities in the work world. I earned a Bachelor's Degree from Fresno State in English Literature in 2011, hoping to become a teacher, but no one was hiring teachers then. The long road from flipping pizzas to writing code has lead me here to Geekwise, where I am working hard to learn everything there is to know about being a front end developer and writing clean, efficient code. My wife tells me that I am happier and more content in general when I am coding, so I take that as a good sign that this is the way I should be headed. Plus, I have actually been able to make more time for my kids since beginning Geekwise than I had before.
